Review quality will be determined via the sole discretion of our chief content coordinator. Which retailers will require the survey form in addition to the review is subject to change at any time. Limit of 200 reviews per year, unless mystery shopper is given explicit permission to write a higher volume of reviews. Reviews will immediately become the intellectual property of American River Gold and will be published on and/or sold to the content platforms of American River Golds choosing and may be sold and shared with partner retailers in addition to potential future partner retailers. Payment for reviews WILL NOT be paid via the Independent Contractors American River Gold Card; it will be paid via check or cash with exact instructions for receiving payment given after review(s) have been submitted. American River Gold Independent Contractors and employees are only allowed to use their American River Gold card for commercial purposes. Non-commercial use is strictly prohibited. Using the American River Gold card for personal, family, or household purposes is strictly prohibited. Also please don't use the card to gamble or fund terrorist activities.
